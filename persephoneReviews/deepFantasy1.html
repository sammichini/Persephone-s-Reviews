Dive Into Deep Fantasy by S U R F I N G

Swimming through S U R F I N G 's premiere album is trippy, romantic, and groovy all at once.
The tone is a lo-fi breath of fresh air and is really nice for relaxing or being peacefully productive.

Tired of Waiting for the Sun

Saxophones featured on this album come at the perfect times.
The whimsical "Moonlight" has to be one of the most glorious tracks on the album.
And it really taps into that inner wolfman.


Somber yet funky "Senegal" unifies synthesizer, bass, and sadness in the most danceable way.
