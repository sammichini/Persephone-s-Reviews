God Is Good

This album features lovely sounds that typically accompany Om's suite, including sitar, cello, and an assortment of percussion.
From the devotional lyrics and transcendent guitar riffs, you will want to pray to something by the end of this album.

Meditation Is The Practice Of Death

This group is inspired by Byzantine culture and Tibetan chants.
Holy energy is well embodied on my favorite track, Meditation is the Practice of Death.
The melody is hypnotic and the flutes bring a necessary lightness to the droning track.
